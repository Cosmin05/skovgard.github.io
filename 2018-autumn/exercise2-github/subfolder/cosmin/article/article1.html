GODSBANEN:
cultural centre, performance venue and much more

So you have just started your adventure as a student in Aarhus. Are you wondering how you can get in touch with the creative side of the city? Or you just want to attend some independently organized workshops or performances. Godsbanen may just be the right answer to your search.

Discover your inner creativeness
Hosting all sorts of open workshops, Godsbanen will definitely help you experience interesting ways of putting that creative skill to work. The workshops offer is truly diverse: from graphic design and sculpture to creative writing and film production. In case you are already a creative professional, why hold your own workshop? More about attending or holding a workshop can be found on Godsbanen’s dedicated website section.

Experience music, literature and performing arts events
At Godsbanen, you can can also experience cool exhibitions and cultural events. The venue hosts a series of stages and spaces – you just need to check the calendar and see which one fits you the best.

You may want to have a drink or something to eat while visiting this awesome place. Rest assured, Godsbanen has you covered – the food you can eat at their restaurant (Spiselauget) is cooked using organic raw products and the prices are student-friendly.

If we made you curious about Godbanen, you can find out more below and on their website. All the materials are available in English as well, so it should be easy for you to check them out


Facts on Godsbanen:
  • • Godsbanen is 10,500 m²
  • • You can walk on the roof; you are 19 meters above ground on the roof;
  • • Approximately 400 events happens at Godsbanen every year
  • • Approximately 175.000 people visits Godsbanen every year
  • • There is free WI-FI. Log on via SMS or NEM-ID.
